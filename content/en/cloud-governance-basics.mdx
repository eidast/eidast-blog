Good governance is not a **gate**; it is a map. Teams move faster when defaults are safe: identity, networking, logging, and cost visibility are boring until they are missing.

Start with a small set of non-negotiables, automate compliance checks in pipelines, and review exceptions in public architecture notes so patterns compound instead of fragment.
