This site is a place for **clear thinking** about enterprise systems: boundaries, platforms, cost, risk, and the politics of shipping software at scale.

I care about:

- Alignment between business outcomes and technical constraints
- Repeatable patterns without dogma
- Honest discussion of trade-offs

More posts will land here as MDX files under `content/en` and `content/es`. Same slug in both folders gives you paired translations for `hreflang`.
